What this layer means

This is the visibility layer of the network. It shows that nodes exist as present entities within an observable surface. It is a continuity signal, not a judgment.

What this layer does not mean

A visible node is not automatically trusted, certified, or endorsed. Formal statements belong to separate evidence, signature, and audit processes.

Join the network
